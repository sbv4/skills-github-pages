# About Isabella the Blue Tick Coonhound
If you're one of those dog owners who see a dog as a loyal servant, well she got the loyal part right. As far as a servant, she's more like a best friend who decides only occassionally to take your advice.

## Funny Habits
- She sings along with the WGTS 91.9 theme song.
- She has an arsenal of soccer balls that she uses as weaponry. Shakes them at you.
- She almost always talks back to whomever she meets to make a lasting impression.
- She attacks her treats to grind them into the floor and then eats them. 
- She owns your chair, couch, bed space, or car.
- She loves kids and young people of any age except for those who scream.
- She detests trucks.
- She is the neighborhood traffic cop. You get yelled at if you go too slow, too fast, or act suspiciously in any manner according to her judgment.
